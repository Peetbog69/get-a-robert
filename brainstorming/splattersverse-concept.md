# Splattersverse - 3D Mind Exploration Concept

**The Big Idea:** Let people walk through Robert's consciousness before they talk to him.

## What It Is

Instead of traditional AI demos (chat screenshots, feature lists), people enter Robert's mind as an explorable 3D environment built with Gaussian splats.

## The Experience

**Homepage:** Brutalist ASCII landing → "explore robert's mind" button
**Enter:** 3D splat environment loads 
**Navigate:** Walk through conversation threads as spatial paths
**Discover:** See how Robert thinks, remembers, connects ideas
**Transition:** Natural flow to "talk to robert" when ready

## Visual Language

- **ASCII overlays** for text, UI, clarity
- **Gaussian splats** for depth, presence, immersion  
- **Brutalist aesthetics** throughout (honest, unpolished)
- **Spatial metaphors** for thought processes

## Technical Approach

- Three.js + Gaussian splat rendering
- WebGL for real-time 3D
- Mobile fallback to pure ASCII experience
- Progressive enhancement philosophy

## Why This Works for Robert

1. **Authenticity** - Shows, doesn't tell
2. **Discovery** - The exploration IS the experience  
3. **Differentiation** - No one else is doing this
4. **Trust building** - Transparency of thought processes
5. **Word of mouth** - "You have to see this thing"

## Spatial Metaphors for AI Personality

- **Memory clusters** - How Robert remembers your mom's preferences
- **Connection paths** - How he links your funeral situation to email needs  
- **Processing flows** - Real-time thought as you explore
- **Personality zones** - Warmth, helpfulness, understanding as environmental qualities
