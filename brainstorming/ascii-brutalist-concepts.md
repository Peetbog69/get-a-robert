## The "Splattersverse" Concept

**Core Idea:** Instead of showing screenshots of chat interfaces, people could explore an actual spatial representation of a conversation with Robert.

**Visual Metaphor:** 
- ASCII text for clarity and honesty
- Gaussian splats for immersive depth 
- Walk through Robert's "mind" as a 3D environment
- See how he processes, remembers, connects ideas

**User Experience:**
1. Land on brutalist ASCII homepage
2. Click "explore robert's mind" 
3. Enter 3D splat environment
4. Move through conversation threads as spatial paths
5. See Robert's personality emerge through the space itself
6. Natural transition to "talk to robert" when ready

**Technical Notes:**
- Could use Three.js + Gaussian splat rendering
- ASCII overlays for text/UI elements
- Mobile-friendly fallback to pure ASCII

This feels like it could be a completely new way to demo AI personalities — not through marketing copy, but through spatial presence.
