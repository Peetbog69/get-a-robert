## How It Works

**Step 1: Pick a name.**
Give it a personality. Name it after someone you like. Your mom named hers Robert. It stuck.

**Step 2: Describe your life.**
What do you need help with? Work tasks? Daily planning? Research? A sounding board? Three questions, five minutes, we figure it out.

**Step 3: Start talking.**
Text it. Voice message it. It responds like a person who knows you. Not a search engine.

**Step 4: That's it.**
No setup. No training. No "learning your workflow." It just works from day one.
