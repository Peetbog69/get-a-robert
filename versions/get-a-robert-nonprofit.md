# An AI That Actually Helps

**For the people who are too busy doing real work to learn new software.**

---

You're running a non-profit. Or a small team. Or you're just the person everyone relies on.

You've tried the AI tools. They're fine for answering questions. But they don't *know* your world. They don't remember your donors, your grant deadlines, your volunteers, your calendar. Every conversation starts from zero.

That's not help. That's another thing to manage.

---

## What This Is

A person in your pocket who actually knows your context. Named by you. Built around your work. No apps. No training. No jargon.

It lives in Telegram — the same app you already text from. You talk to it like a person. It talks back like one. And it remembers what you told it last week.

---

## What It Looks Like

- **"Can you draft an email to the Johnson family about the spring fundraiser?"** — Done. In your voice.
- **"Remind me to follow up with the grant officer on Thursday."** — Done.
- **"Summarize the last three board meeting notes."** — Done. In bullet points.
- **"I'm overwhelmed. Help me plan tomorrow."** — Done.

It doesn't replace you. It handles the overhead so you can do the actual work.

---

## Try It

Text a bot. Talk to it for two hours. No credit card, no commitment.

If it helps, keep it. If not, no hard feelings.

Most people say yes to that. And most people end up keeping it.
