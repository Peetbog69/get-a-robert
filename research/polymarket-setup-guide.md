## What We're Setting Up

1. **Coinbase** — where you buy USDC with your bank
2. **MetaMask (wallet)** — where you hold your crypto

**Budget:** ~$100 USDC + ~$2 MATIC (for gas)
**Network:** Polygon (not Ethereum mainnet)

## Step 1: Install MetaMask (Wallet)

### Why you need this
MetaMask is a browser extension that holds your crypto. You control the keys — nobody else can touch your money. Think of it as your own personal crypto bank account that only you have the password to.

### Instructions

1. **Go to** [metamask.io](https://metamask.io)
2. **Click** "Download" — make sure it's the official site
3. **Install** the browser extension (Chrome, Firefox, or Brave)
4. **Click** "Create a new wallet"
5. **Set a strong password** — not the same as your email password
6. **WRITE DOWN YOUR SEED PHRASE** on paper. Not a screenshot. Not in a text file. **On paper.** This is the master key to your money. If you lose it, nobody can help you recover it.
7. **Never share this phrase with anyone** — not even me

### Important
- Your wallet will default to Ethereum network. We'll add Polygon next.
- Store that seed phrase somewhere safe before doing anything else.

---

## Step 2: Add Polygon Network to MetaMask

### Instructions

1. **Open MetaMask** (click the fox icon in your browser toolbar)
2. **Click** the network dropdown at the top (it probably says "Ethereum Mainnet")
3. **Click** "Add Network"
4. **Fill in these details:**
   - **Network Name:** Polygon Mainnet
   - **RPC URL:** https://polygon-rpc.com
   - **Chain ID:** 137
   - **Symbol:** MATIC
   - **Block Explorer URL:** https://polygonscan.com
5. **Click** Save

You should now see "Polygon Mainnet" in your network list. Switch to it.

---

## Step 3: Buy MATIC (for gas fees)

You need about $2 worth of MATIC for gas fees. This is what pays the network to process your transactions.

### Option A: Buy directly in MetaMask (easiest)
1. In MetaMask, switch to Polygon network
2. Click "Buy"
3. Follow the prompts to buy MATIC — MetaMask uses Coinbase Pay or Transak
4. Buy ~$2 worth (this will last you dozens of transactions)

### Option B: Buy from an exchange (if you prefer)
1. Buy MATIC on Coinbase or Kraken
2. Withdraw to your MetaMask wallet address on the **Polygon network**
3. Always double-check you're sending on Polygon, not Ethereum

## Step 4: Sign Up for Coinbase

### Instructions

1. **Go to** [coinbase.com](https://coinbase.com)
2. **Click** "Get started"
3. **Use your email:** shortbuslove@protonmail.com
4. **Verify your identity** — this requires:
   - Government ID (driver's license, passport)
   - Selfie (they compare your face to the ID)
   - Phone number
   - Address
5. **This step takes about 5-10 minutes.** Coinbase is a regulated US company — this is normal.

### Why this matters
Coinbase is where your real money meets crypto. Your bank connects here. You want them to know who you are.

---

## Step 5: Buy USDC on Coinbase

### Instructions

1. **Log into** Coinbase
2. **Click** "Buy / Sell"
3. **Search for** "USDC"
4. **Enter** $100
5. **Select** your funding source (bank account / debit card)
   - Bank account (ACH): free but takes 2-3 days
   - Debit card: instant but small fee (~$3)
6. **Confirm** the purchase

Now you own 100 USDC — each worth exactly $1.

---

## Step 6: Send USDC to Your Wallet

### Instructions

1. **Open Coinbase** → go to your USDC balance
2. **Click** "Send" or "Withdraw"
3. **Paste** your MetaMask wallet address:
   - Open MetaMask, make sure you're on Polygon network
   - Your address is under your account name (starts with 0x...)
   - Click it to copy
4. **CRITICAL — Select the right network:**
   - Network dropdown → **Polygon**
   - NOT Ethereum. NOT Solana. **Polygon.**
5. **Enter** the amount: $100
6. **Review** the details one more time
7. **Confirm**

### ⚠️ Warning
If you send USDC on the wrong network (Ethereum instead of Polygon), the funds can still be recovered but it costs $20-50 in gas and takes work. Always triple-check the network.

---

## Step 7: Connect Wallet to Polymarket

### Instructions

1. **Go to** [polymarket.com](https://polymarket.com)
2. **Click** "Connect Wallet"
3. **Select** MetaMask from the options
4. **Approve** the connection in the MetaMask popup
5. **Done** — you should see your USDC balance in Polymarket

---

## Step 8: Place Your First Bet

### Instructions

1. **Browse** markets on Polymarket
2. **Find one** you have an opinion on
3. **Click** "Buy Yes" or "Buy No"
4. **Enter** the amount of USDC you want to risk (start small — $10-20 for your first)
5. **Review** the price and potential payout
6. **Confirm** the transaction in MetaMask
7. **Approve** the gas fee (should be < $0.01 on Polygon)

---

## Step 9: Cashing Out (When You're Ready)

### Instructions

1. **On Polymarket**, click your balance → "Withdraw"
2. **Send** USDC back to your MetaMask wallet (if it's not already there)
3. **On Coinbase** → "Receive" → select USDC → **Polygon** network
4. **In MetaMask** → send USDC to the Coinbase deposit address
5. **On Coinbase** → sell USDC for USD (free)
6. **Withdraw** USD to your bank account via ACH (free, 1-2 days)

**Total cost to exit:** ~$0.01 in gas. That's it.
