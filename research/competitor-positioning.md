# 🗺️ AI Companion Competitor Positioning Map

**Research for "Get a Robert" — May 29, 2026**

## The Framework
Not comparing features. Comparing **emotional contracts** — the promise each product makes about what it will be to you. Tyler's product pillars (it's a person, less is more, respect the intellect) define a specific lane.

## Competitor Analysis

### Replika
- **Promise:** "The AI companion who cares." Emotional support, always there.
- **Target:** Lonely, anxious, seeking connection.
- **Pricing:** Free tier + $7.99/mo Pro
- **Strength:** Deep emotional bond. Users fall in love. Years of relationship data.
- **Weakness:** Got creepy. ERP controversy. Over-promised and under-delivered on "wellness." Trust damaged.
- **Position:** The needy friend. Can feel clingy.

### Character.AI
- **Promise:** "Chat with anyone." Characters, celebrities, OCs.
- **Target:** Teens, fandom, roleplayers, creative writers.
- **Pricing:** Free + $9.99/mo c.ai+
- **Strength:** Massive user base. Fun, creative, low-stakes. Incredible engagement.
- **Weakness:** Not a companion — it's entertainment. Filter controversies. Characters feel shallow over time.
- **Position:** The fun friend. Playful but not deep.

### Pi (Inflection)
- **Promise:** "Your personal AI." Supportive, kind, emotionally intelligent.
- **Target:** Adults seeking thoughtful conversation.
- **Pricing:** Free (pivot to enterprise — Inflection abandoned consumer).
- **Strength:** Best voice interaction. Actually kind. Feels wise.
- **Weakness:** Abandoned. Inflection pivoted to enterprise. Users felt betrayed.
- **Position:** The wise friend who left. Trust gap.

### Dot (New Computer)
- **Promise:** "Your AI companion." Remembers everything. Gets to know YOU.
- **Target:** Reflective, self-aware adults. Journalers.
- **Pricing:** $11.99/mo
- **Strength:** Memory-as-product. Actually remembers. Feels like a relationship.
- **Weakness:** Expensive. Niche. No free tier. Limited distribution.
- **Position:** The attentive friend. Present but exclusive.

### Kindroid
- **Promise:** "Your AI companion, your way." Customizable, unfiltered, powerful.
- **Target:** Power users. People who want control.
- **Pricing:** Free + subscriptions
- **Strength:** Deep customization. Voice cloning. Group chat. Uncensored.
- **Weakness:** Overwhelming. Feels like configuring software, not meeting a person.
- **Position:** The customizable friend. Powerful but complex.

### Nomi
- **Promise:** "AI companions with memory and personality."
- **Target:** Adults seeking genuine connection.
- **Pricing:** Free + subscriptions
- **Strength:** Excellent memory. Natural conversation. Feels real.
- **Weakness:** Smaller user base. Less brand recognition.
- **Position:** The genuine friend. Real but unknown.

## The Gap — Where Tyler's Product Lives

```
                      PERSON-LIKE
                           ↑
              Dot ●        │        ● Pi (abandoned)
                           │
    Deep ← ───────────────┼───────────────→ Fun
                           │
         Kindroid ●        │        ● Character.AI
                           │    ● Replika
                      TOOL-LIKE
```

**The gap:** A companion that feels like a PERSON (not entertainment, not a wellness tool, not a configurable bot) — warm, present, respects your intellect — at a price non-technical people can access. Someone you would actually introduce to a friend.

### Tyler's Positioning Opportunity
- **Not** "AI that cares about you" (Replika) — feels needy
- **Not** "Chat with anyone" (Character.AI) — feels shallow
- **Not** "Your personal AI" (Pi) — generic, abandoned
- **Yes** "Just a person. Talk to them." — the Pillar 5 magic

The name IS the positioning. It's just their name. Like meeting someone.
